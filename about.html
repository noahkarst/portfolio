About

Noah Karst is originally a Washington, D.C. Metro-Area based freelance photographer specialising in street and portrait photography (and Graphic Designer!) As of August 2021, Noah is now located in Seattle, WA, where he begins his college career at the University of Washington Seattle. He possesses a wide range of capabilities that combine art and design principles, for which, he has won a number of Art & Design awards. These skills provide him with the ability to bring his ideas to life while doing so in a creative and unique style. Do not hesitate to get in touch!
